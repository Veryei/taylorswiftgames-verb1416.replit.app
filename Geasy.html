Fun fact #10

The very first song Taylor Swift ever wrote was called "Lucky You." She wrote it when she was 12 years old after a computer repairman who came to her house taught her how to play three chords on a guitar.

Fun fact #11

"Love Story" is Taylor's best-selling single to date, selling well over 5 million copies.

Fun fact #12

"All Too Well" was the first song Taylor wrote for her fourth studio album, Red.
