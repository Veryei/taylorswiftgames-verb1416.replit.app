Fun fact #13

For Taylor the number 13 is lucky for a number of reasons. In 2009 she told MTV: "I was born on the 13th. I turned 13 on Friday the 13th. My first album went gold in 13 weeks. My first Number 1 song had a 13-second intro. Every time I've won an award I've been seated in either the 13th seat, the 13th row, the 13th section or row M, which is the 13th letter."

Fun fact #14

Taylor got the idea for "You Belong With Me" after hearing a friend of hers getting screamed at over the phone by his girlfriend.

Fun fact #15

In its debut week, Red sold approximately two albums every second.
